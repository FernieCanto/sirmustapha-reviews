Q: Are you an Arab?
A: No! I live in South America, in the country popularly known as Brazil; at the very South, in a town called Porto Alegre. It's one of the best towns in the world, by the way.

Q: So, why the nickname 'Sir Mustapha'?
A: Because of the universally famous Queen hit-single 'Mustapha'. I came up with it by the time I was making my AOL screen name (sic!), and since there was a "Mustapha" already, I stuck in "Sir" in front of it for a change. And then it stuck.

Q: What's your real name, then?

Q: What do you do for a living?
A: At the moment, I just finished a Technical Course in computer programming, and I have a job in the university. I'm putting the knowledge I acquired on the course into practice, and it's quite nice. Plus, I'm having financial return from it. Could I ask for more? Sure.

Q: What motivated you to make a reviews page?
A: Well, being a musician myself, I love talking about music. And I like expressing my opinions. So, I think it would be interesting if I exposed my feelings on music to whomever is concerned - since everyone's doing it anyway. Also... I didn't want to waste my Web space in Geocities.

Q: Are you really a musician?
A: Pretty much - that is, if you're not a bloody elitist who thinks it's not enough to produce 3 CDs worth of original music and think you need to make Music College to be called a musician. But I think I am. I play the keyboards myself, and I have been writing music in the computer for quite some time. It's all instrumental music played mostly on synthesized MIDI sounds, and my albums (Musics For Highways, The Binary Sounds Of Nature and Concerto Schizophrenia II: "Buses") are available for FREE DOWNLOAD on my MP3 site. Download them.

Q: What kinds of music do you like?
A: Well, I like good music. Forgive me for the unforgivable cliché, but that's pretty much true. I can like mostly any genre of music, provided it's good, and displays talent and originality. I love rock music, I like reggae (Bob Marley's da king, mon), I like good Brazilian samba, I like some experimental stuff, some avant-garde stuff... well, you know the drift.

Q: What are your favourite bands?
A: Well, the band that's responsible for bringing me into liking music is Queen, via their Greatest Hits CD. From there, I can mention The Beatles and The Who; Pink Floyd, Yes, King Crimson, Rush, The Moody Blues and all that prog art rock crap; some "novelty" acts like Wire, Captain Beefheart and the Magic Band, The Residents; and solo artists like Ben Arion, Brain One, Ben O'rian and, of course, Brian Eno; Mike Oldfield, Jean-Michel Jarre... and there's also Radiohead, my favourite 90's band. Presto. I think I covered it all.

Q: What's with the little quotes on top of your review pages?
A: Those are quotes I made up myself (most times), relative to how I think the artists behave. They're just there for a comical/humourous appeal (most times), and are not meant to be taken seriously.

Q: And what's with the South Park designs, anyway?
A: Those are part of a little joke. Since I didn't have any band photos, I decided to make my own using a nifty South Park Create-A-Character you can find here.

Q: What else do you do aside from half-arsed music reviews?
A: Well, aside from composing music and writing about music, I like to write and read fanfiction for the best television comedy series of all time, The Simpsons. Yeah, I used to watch the show religiously before it hit rock bottom with seasons 13 and 14, and I was responsible for some fanfiction, too. I belong to a group of friends who go under the name of the Groening FanWorks Central, formed by people who write and draw for The Simpsons and Futurama. They're great people, and they accept your fanfiction if it's good and if you post it there. Give the website a look. We are all nerds, of course, but we're varied enough.

Q: So, you are a fan of Lisa Simpson, anyway?
A: Absolutely. I could go on for hours talking about good ol' Lisa, but suffice to say, I think she's the best character to grace television. That's just me, of course, but it is what I think. My heart is filled with joy and love whenever I hear her name or see her on the (computer or television) screen. She just plain rocks.
